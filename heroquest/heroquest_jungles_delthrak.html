Jungles of Delthrak

Jungles of Delthrak is a bold new HeroQuest quest pack announced for release in 2025. It takes adventurers deep into a lost dwarven civilization swallowed by vines, beasts, and time. Traps are everywhere. Civilization is long gone. But treasure, secrets, and terror remain. For the first time, the game introduces two brand-new playable heroes designed specifically for jungle exploration and brutal survival: the Berserker and the Explorer.

Story Overview

Delthrak was once a dwarven kingdom built in harmony with the surrounding jungle. But long ago, the capital fell to a curse. Now the jungle has grown wild, and creatures corrupted by ancient magic guard the ruins. Zargon seeks to awaken something buried beneath the roots. The heroes must journey into the green hell to stop him and uncover the truth of Delthrak’s fall.

What’s in the Box

  • Quest Book with 15 new missions
  • Jungle terrain tiles: vines, rivers, quicksand, stone temples
  • New monsters: Jungle Serpents, Cursebound, Temple Guardians, Primordial Horrors
  • 2 New Playable Heroes: The Berserker and the Explorer

The Berserker

The Berserker is a raw melee force who trades defense for massive damage. He thrives on chaos and frenzy. The more he's hurt, the stronger he becomes.

  • Best used as a front-line disruptor
  • Can ignore fear or magical illusions
  • Cannot wear heavy armor — favors mobility and rage

The Explorer

The Explorer is a savvy, agile adventurer who excels at finding hidden paths, avoiding danger, and navigating terrain. She is perfect for jungle environments full of hazards and lost treasures.

  • Excels in trap-heavy and labyrinthine areas
  • Useful for bypassing enemies or flanking
  • Low attack power but high agility

Design and Narrative Highlights

  • Quests emphasize environmental navigation and danger
  • Branching paths: some areas can only be reached by the Explorer
  • Berserker unlocks alternate fight paths with brute force
  • Final battle includes terrain collapse and moving hazards

Real-World Analogy: Indiana Jones Meets Conan the Barbarian

Imagine Tomb Raider or Uncharted mashed up with Jungle Book and Conan. This campaign is all about lost civilizations, raw combat, deadly plants, and ancient traps. It's sweaty, vine-draped, and packed with forgotten horrors and glittering artifacts.

Summary

Jungles of Delthrak reinvents HeroQuest as an environmental survival experience. With the addition of the Berserker and Explorer, parties can tackle challenges with new playstyles and adapt to traps, twists, and jungle terrain. It’s a wild, dangerous, and richly thematic expansion.
