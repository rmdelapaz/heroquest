HeroQuest Lore Overview

World at a Glance

Avalonia—often called “the Realm”—is a tapestry of rugged frontiers, dwarven mountain holds, elven highlands, and shadow‑veiled wastelands. Think continental Europe mashed with high‑fantasy Middle‑earth: forests echo with elven ballads while distant volcanoes glow with Chaos.

Key Factions & Figures

  • Mentor (formerly Rogar): Sage of the Order, guiding heroes from his tower.
  • Zargon (a.k.a. Morcar): Fallen apprentice turned dark overlord scheming from the Northlands.
  • Emperor: Human monarch of the Eastern Empire; besieged in Kellar’s Keep.
  • Witch Lord: Undying necromancer raising armies from Kaleth’s crypts.
  • Frozen Horror: Yeti‑god entombed beneath polar ice, now awakened.
  • Sinestra: Mirror mage manipulating elven politics.
  • Gurbash the Cruel: Ogre warlord leading frontier raids.

Major Locations

  • Karak Varn Mountains: Dwarven citadels and the besieged fortress Kellar’s Keep.
  • Kaleth: Haunted city of mausoleums where the Witch Lord stirs.
  • Northlands: Frozen tundra home to ice caverns and mercenary outposts.
  • Elven Kingdom of Elethorn: Verdant woods hiding mirror portals and rogue assassins.
  • Delthrak Jungle: Overgrown ruins concealing lost dwarven forges and reptilian tribes.
  • Andor: Human frontier threatened by Dread Moon cultists.

Villain Motivations

• Zargon/Morcar: Seeks revenge on Mentor and mastery of Chaos magic.
• Witch Lord: Believes undeath is the path to eternal power.
• Frozen Horror: Primal force of entropy—wants the world encased in ice.
• Sinestra: Desires to shatter elven society via identity manipulation.
• Dread Moon Cult: Worship cosmic entities behind the red eclipse.
• Gurbash: Ogrelike hunger for conquest and glory.
