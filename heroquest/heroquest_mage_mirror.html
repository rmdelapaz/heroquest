The Mage of the Mirror

The Mage of the Mirror is the Elf Quest Pack, originally released in 1992 alongside The Frozen Horror. Like its counterpart, it placed one classic Hero in the spotlight ‚Äî this time, the Elf. A tale of magical corruption, royal kidnapping, and mirror-travel unfolds across icy peaks, enchanted cities, and fae-glimmering forests.

Story Overview

All is not well in the elven kingdom of Elethorn. The rightful princess has been kidnapped, and a powerful High Mage has usurped her identity using forbidden mirror magic. The Elf hero must travel alone at first to uncover the truth, reclaim the princess, and stop the spreading dark enchantment.

What's in the Box

  • Quest Book with 10 multiplayer quests + 3 Elf solo quests
  • New monsters: Elven Archers, Ogres, Giant Wolves
  • New tiles: mirrors, ice walls, magical portals, fae terrain
  • New elf-specific equipment and spells

Unique Gameplay Features

  • Solo quests: The Elf begins the story alone, deepening character immersion
  • Portal-based maps: Players must use magical mirrors to navigate key areas
  • Magical puzzles: More emphasis on thought over brute force
  • Ogre Enemies: Large, high-damage brutes require careful tactics

Hero Spotlight: The Elf

The Elf, often balanced between sword and sorcery, takes the stage here as a full protagonist. She gains access to new elven artifacts, magical items, and lore unique to the questline. Her ability to combine melee and magic becomes essential against puzzle-based obstacles and enemies that resist standard attacks.

Real-World Analogy: High Fantasy & Intrigue

If The Frozen Horror is a Norse saga, then Mage of the Mirror is an elven court drama meets magical mystery. Think Labyrinth crossed with Princess Mononoke ‚Äî beauty, betrayal, and brain-teasers wrapped in high fantasy.

Design Highlights

  • Solo quests build narrative agency and personal connection
  • Mirrors and portals redefine how rooms are explored
  • Encounters vary between logic, ranged duels, and brute-force battles
  • Final battle includes multi-phase mechanics

Summary

The Mage of the Mirror is an elegant, challenging quest pack that elevates the Elf hero and introduces more intellectual and magical depth. It completes the classic cycle of hero-focused adventures while offering a satisfying blend of puzzles, beauty, and dark magic.
