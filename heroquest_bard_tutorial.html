Meet the Bard

The Bard is HeroQuest's embodiment of inspirational leadership and versatile support - imagine a Renaissance polymath who combines the motivational power of a great general, the knowledge of a scholar, and the adaptability of a diplomat. Where other heroes excel through personal power, the Bard transforms ordinary adventurers into legendary heroes through music, knowledge, and the ancient arts of inspiration.
