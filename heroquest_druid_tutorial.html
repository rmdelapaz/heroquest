Meet the Druid

The Druid is HeroQuest's embodiment of primal natural power - imagine a forest guardian who channels the raw forces of earth, wind, fire, and water while maintaining deep harmony with the living world. Where other heroes rely on crafted weapons or scholarly magic, the Druid draws power directly from nature itself, wielding elemental forces and shapeshifting abilities that make them uniquely adaptable to any environment.
