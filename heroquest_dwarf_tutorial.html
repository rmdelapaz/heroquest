Meet the Dwarf

The Dwarf is HeroQuest's ultimate utility specialist - imagine a medieval Swiss Army knife in the form of a bearded, axe-wielding engineer. While others charge into combat or cast spells, the Dwarf excels at the dangerous technical work that keeps the party alive: detecting deadly traps, disarming cunning mechanisms, and finding hidden treasures that others would miss entirely.

Core Statistics: The Balanced Specialist

Movement: 2d6 (2-12 squares, avg 7)
Attack: 2 combat dice
Defend: 2 combat dice
Body Points: 7
Mind Points: 3
Think of it this way: If heroes were professionals, the Dwarf would be a master craftsman - reliable in all areas but truly exceptional at specialized tasks. Like a skilled electrician who can handle basic carpentry but truly shines when working with complex wiring, the Dwarf can fight and survive, but excels at the technical challenges of dungeon exploration.

Combat Capabilities: The Reliable Warrior

With 2 attack dice and 2 defend dice, the Dwarf sits comfortably in the middle of HeroQuest's combat spectrum. They're not the devastating force of the Barbarian, but they're far from helpless, and their 7 Body Points provide solid survivability.

Military Analogy: The Dwarf is like a combat engineer - not the front-line infantry (Barbarian) or the artillery specialist (Wizard), but the essential support personnel who can fight effectively when needed while providing crucial technical expertise.

Equipment Strategy: Tools of the Trade

The Dwarf's equipment choices should enhance their utility role while providing adequate combat capability. Unlike the Barbarian who focuses purely on combat gear, the Dwarf needs a more diverse toolkit.

Optimal Equipment Priority

Primary Weapons: Battle Axe or Short Sword - reliable damage without sacrificing utility

Secondary Tools: Crossbow for ranged support and kiting dangerous enemies

Protective Gear: Leather Armor for basic protection without mobility penalties

Utility Items: Rope, tools, and healing potions for extended exploration

Philosophy: Jack-of-all-trades loadout that supports the party's overall success rather than maximizing personal combat power
