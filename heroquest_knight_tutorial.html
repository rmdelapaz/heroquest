Meet the Knight

The Knight is HeroQuest's embodiment of disciplined martial excellence and unwavering honor - imagine a medieval paladin who combines the tactical precision of a master strategist with the protective instincts of a devoted guardian. Where other warriors fight for glory or gold, the Knight stands as an unbreakable wall between evil and innocence, wielding superior armor, refined combat techniques, and an unshakeable code of honor that transforms mere fighting into an art form.

Core Statistics: The Armored Fortress

Movement: 2d6 (2-12 squares, avg 7)
Attack: 3 combat dice
Defend: 3 combat dice
Body Points: 8
Mind Points: 3
Think of it this way: If heroes were military units, the Knight would be a main battle tank - heavily armored (3 Defend, 8 Body), powerfully armed (3 Attack), with sophisticated targeting systems (3 Mind for tactical thinking) but requiring careful positioning and support to maximize effectiveness. Like a tank, the Knight dominates through superior protection and firepower rather than speed or stealth.
