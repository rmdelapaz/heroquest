üìú The Determinist View: "Everything is written in the stars"

  • Comfort in knowing ultimate victory is assured
  • Risk of fatalism and lack of personal agency

‚ö° The Free Will View: "We forge our own destiny"

  • Heroes' choices determine which future comes to pass
  • Empowerment through personal responsibility
  • Burden of potential failure and consequences

‚öñÔ∏è The Synthesis View: "Destiny provides the stage, we write the play"

  • Major events are fated, but details depend on choices
  • Heroes have agency within cosmic parameters
  • Balance between security and freedom
  • Emphasis on how destiny is fulfilled, not whether
