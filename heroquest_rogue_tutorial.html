Meet the Rogue

The Rogue is HeroQuest's embodiment of cunning, adaptability, and surgical precision - imagine a master thief crossed with a spy, an assassin, and a con artist who thrives in the spaces between heroism and villainy. Where other heroes face challenges head-on, the Rogue finds the hidden path, the secret weakness, the unexpected angle that turns impossible odds into calculated advantages. They are the shadow that moves unseen, the whisper that reveals secrets, and the blade that strikes from nowhere.
