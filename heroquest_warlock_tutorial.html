Meet the Warlock

The Warlock is HeroQuest's embodiment of corrupted magical power - imagine a Wizard who has traded pure arcane study for raw, chaotic energy drawn from dark sources. Where the Wizard represents scholarly mastery, the Warlock channels primal forces that are both more immediately powerful and far more dangerous to wield. This hero walks the razor's edge between devastating effectiveness and self-destruction.

Core Statistics: The Dark Hybrid

Movement: 2d6 (2-12 squares, avg 7)
Attack: 2 combat dice
Defend: 2 combat dice
Body Points: 5
Mind Points: 5
Think of it this way: If heroes were academic disciplines, the Warlock would be a brilliant researcher who abandoned ethics for results. Like a scientist who conducts dangerous experiments without safety protocols, the Warlock gains power through methods that put them at constant risk. They're more physically capable than a pure Wizard, but their magic comes with a terrible price.
