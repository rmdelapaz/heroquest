Meet the Wizard

The Wizard is HeroQuest's embodiment of pure magical power - imagine Gandalf crossed with a medieval artillery unit, wielding reality-bending spells while being as fragile as ancient parchment. This hero represents the ultimate high-risk, high-reward playstyle: capable of devastating entire groups of enemies or solving impossible problems with magic, but requiring constant protection and careful resource management to survive.

Core Statistics: The Glass Cannon

Movement: 2d6 (2-12 squares, avg 7)
Attack: 1 combat die
Defend: 2 combat dice
Body Points: 4
Mind Points: 6
Think of it this way: If heroes were military units, the Wizard would be a mobile artillery platform - devastating firepower (6 Mind Points) but extremely vulnerable (4 Body Points, 1 Attack). Like a howitzer that can level buildings but needs infantry protection, the Wizard can reshape battles but crumbles under direct assault.
